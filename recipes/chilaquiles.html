Chilaquiles

Description

Indulge in a culinary fiesta with our mouthwatering recipe for delicious Chilaquiles. This traditional Mexican dish takes your breakfast or brunch to a whole new level of satisfaction. With a delightful blend of flavors and textures, Chilaquiles are guaranteed to ignite your taste buds and transport you to the sunny streets of Mexico.

Ingredients:

  • 3 Large Tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup Chopped Onion
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 4 Sprigs of Oregano
  • 12 Corn Tortillas
  • Crumbled Queso Frescog

Instructions:

  1. For the sauce, chop up the tomatoes, onion, and oregano
  2. Toss them into the blender until liquified
  3. Cut the tortillas into thin strips and fry them in oil until crispy
  4. Pour the sauce into the used oil, along with tortilla strips
  5. Stir for around 5 minutes, then serve with cheese for topping
